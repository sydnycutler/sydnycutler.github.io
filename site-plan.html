Overview

Purpose

We strive to enrich people's lives by providing outstanding adventures. Our goal is to help people of all ages enjoy the best outdoor boating experience of their lives. We personally encourage and actively support awareness, deeper appreciation and preservation of our natural ecosystems. Our trip emphasize fun, education, and boat safety.

Audience

Ages can range from youth to adults, both male and female. My audience enjoys outdoor activities, adventure, swimming, and a bit little danger. They generally are outgoing or extroverts looking for new ways to learn and have fun.

Typography

Heading Font: Lobster Two

It is a very close font to the font on my logo and I would like for things to match so that I don't have so many different looks.

Paragraph Font: Montserrat

My heading font is cursive and I wanted a sans-serif to compliment it. I also liked how this text spreads out a little more than the Lobster Two font. It gives a good contrast.

Normal paragraph example

The best Whitewater Rafting in Colorado, the Chocolate River Rafting Company offers rafting on the Colorado and Roaring Fork Rivers in Glenwood Springs. Since 1974, we have been family owned and operated, rafting the Shoshone section of Glenwood Canyon and beyond.
