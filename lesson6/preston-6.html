Halloween fun fills the week in Preston

By Thaya Gilmore

Throughout October, it is easy to get the Halloween holiday spirit right in Franklin County. Preston City businesses are hosting a free and safe Halloween night, Wed., Oct. 31, starting at 4:30 p.m. to 6 p.m. Businesses from Stokes Marketplace to Big J’s Burgers up State Street are handing out treats, providing games, activities and photo opportunities on the sidewalk in front of their business.
