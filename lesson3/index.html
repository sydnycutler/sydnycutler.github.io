Purpose

The 4caster appeals to the uncommon audience. Those of us who may think that weather is only for parents or older people. The 4caster brings a fun, new way of looking at the weather. It gives more relatable content that is easy to read. And creates a personalized experience for younger generations. We use easy navigation so that our users can get their information quickly and without hassle. We promise to give accurate and updated information about the weather so you can plan ahead.
