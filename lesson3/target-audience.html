Description

Teenagers to Young Adults

Ages 16 to 30

Male and female

Those who have no interest in the weather.

Spending most of their time inside; watching YouTube videos and looking at memes.

Personas

Scenarios

Maddie

Zac

Hunter
Maddie is a 22 year old girl. She love Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube. She loves to watch dance videos and makeup and hair tutorials. She currently lives with 3 other roommates. And is a junior in college. She also works part-time as a cashier at Forever 21. Zac is a 17 year old guy. He love hanging out with friends, playing video games, and watching people play video games YouTube. He currently lives with his parents and two younger brothers. He is senior in high school and because he is involved in extracurricular activities like football, he doesn't have a job. Hunter is a 26 year old guy. He likes watching skateboard videos online, and spends a lot of his time on Instagram. He lives by himself but has a dog. He is in his masters program for mathematics. He is also interning as TA for one of his professors.
  • Maddie just did her hair and is planning on a night of fun with her friends. She needs to know if her hair is going to hold up in the weather.
  • Maddie is creating a new Tiktok but plans on doing it outside tomorrow. She needs to know how the lighting is going to be. Therefore needing to check the weather.
  • Maddie knows that fall is coming, but doesn't know when. She needs to check the weather to see when she can pull out her sweaters.
  • Maddie wants to create a makeup look for her Instagram but she is want the image to be sort of dark and gloomy. She needs to check the weather for this image.
  • Maddie is getting ready for work, but doesn't know what to wear. She should check the weather.
  • Zac is getting ready for his football game tomorrow, he wants to take clothes that will match up with the weather.
  • Zac thinks that checking the weather is dumb, but needs to check. He knows that our site appeals to a young audience.
  • Zac invites his friend over, but he needs to check the weather to see if they will be outside or inside.
  • Zac is watching a livestream of one of his favor gamers. They are reenacting one of his favorite video games. But all of the sudden, it starts to hail in the video. Zac thinks that they should have checked the weather.
  • Zac is getting ready for school. The weather has been off and on the past few days so he decides to check the weather.
  • Hunter is going to the skate park. He checks the weather to make sure the park won't be wet and slippery.
  • Hunter takes his dog on a walk everyday. It was sunny in the morning but now there are dark clouds. He checks the weather, just in case.
  • Hunter decides to go home and spend time with his family. They live about 2 hours away and he want to ride his motorcycle. He checks the weather so he will be safe on his ride.
  • Hunter has been tasked with the field trip for the class. He needs to check the weather on that day to see if it would be okay to be outside or not.
  • Hunter scrolls through Instagram and sees people enjoying the outdoors. He wants to get out of his house and do the same. He checks the weather to feel prepared for his adventure.
